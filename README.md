#### Glass runs in background, sees your screen and listens to your audio.


- `Ctrl/Cmd + Enter` to Ask AI with your context anytime.
- `Listen` starts note taking and live summary.
- `Ctrl/Cmd + \` to hide, `Ctrl/Cmd + arrows` to move.
